How to Download OllaCart Extension

Step 1: Download the Extension

Visit the Chrome Web Store to download the OllaCart extension. This extension allows you to add products to OllaCart from any site.

Step 2: Pin Extension to Chrome Search Bar and Sign In

Click on the Puzzle icon on your Chrome browser, and then click on the Pin icon to keep OllaCart handy.

Sign in to your OllaCart account at www.ollacart.com to automatically link your account to your extension.

Step 3: Shop Online as Usual

Shop online on any website as you normally would, just select the OllaCart Extension to add to OllaCart.


How to Add an Item to OllaCart Using the Extension

Step 1: Select the OllaCart Logo

Select the OllaCart logo from your search bar to add a product to your OllaCart.

Step 2: Confirm Product Information

Maneuver the cursor so that the product you want shows appropriate information in the OllaCart popup. Click when the product information is most accurate.

Step 3: Manually select information (Optional)

If necessary, select "Manual Select" to change the information. Make sure the price, description, main logo, images, and product name are correct by maneuvering the cursor when prompted for specific information, and then clicking once.

Manually Selecting information one piece of information at a time should be a single click per item experience, and you can finish at any time by clicking "Finish", knowing that the prompted information is still saved.

Step 4: Add to OllaCart

Once the information is confirmed, select "Looks Correct" to add this product to your OllaCart.


How to Use OllaCart to Purchase and Share Items

1: How to Purchase an Item

Making the outline green by clicking once shows the item in your purchase cart.

Navigate to the Purchase Cart and select "Purchase Items" to purchase multiple items from multiple sites at once.

Step 2: Add item to Share Cart

Making the outline blue by clicking twice shares the item on your public shopping cart web page, as well as with other OllaCart users.

Step 3: Share a Purchased Item

Clicking three times, meaning it is in both Purchase and Share Carts, and then purchasing the item will share that you have purchased the item, keeping the item in your Shared Cart.

Step 4: Manage Items

To delete items, add items from another users cart, or share items via email, use the appropriate icons which are also within the quickview page.

You can trace back to the website where the product came to get more information by visiting the link within the quickview page or selecting the link logo from your home cart.


Tips for using OllaCart

Tip 1: Visit the Account Page and Toggle Security

Toggle the "Secure my Account" Toggle to require email authentication on each login.

Tip 2: Include Promotional Codes in every Purchase

Visit the Account Page and toggle Promo Codes.

Promo codes are an integral part of feeling like you got the best deal on an online purchase. That is why we do all the heavy work of finding promo codes and ensuring you get the best deal automatically.

Toggle the "Include Promo Codes" toggle and refer a friend to activate every promo code and saving opportunity.

Tip 3: Provide Likes and Dislikes to your friends items

The social feed is an amazing way to discover products as well as shop socially online.

Refer friends to OllaCart that you want to shop online with by visiting the account page and entering their email.

Once they have signed up, search for their account name within the Social Cart to shop online together with OllaCart.

Tip 4: Edit the Product Description within OllaCart

Let someone know what you are thinking about a product by editing the description within OllaCart quickview.

Edit the description by selecting the pen icon within the products quick view. Don't forget to save!


Make Online Shopping Easier

Step 1: Save Shipping Information in OllaCart

Save shipping information within the Account Page, which will automatically populate to your purchase.

Step 2: Include taxes in the price estimates.

Knowing how much you need to spend for an item including taxes makes online shopping less full of suprises.

Step 3: Purchase items Anonymously

Purchasing items anonymously means the retailer doesnt get your credit card information or your email. Do this by toggling "Anonymous Purchase" within the account page.

Step 4: Utilize Link

Store credit card information securely and purchase anything from anywhere at once with phone number authentication via OllaCart.

Step 5: Track your purchases using OllaCart

Keep your shipping and purchase history information in one secure place on OllaCart.
