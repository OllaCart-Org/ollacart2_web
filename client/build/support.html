TERMS OF USE

1. Download the Extension

Visit the Chrome Web Store to download the extension. This will allow you to add products or items to OllaCart from any site with two clicks of your mouse.

2. Download the Extension

Visit the Chrome Web Store, and search OllaCart. Download our extension. To make it easy to access on your browswer, click the Puzzle Icon on your Chrome Browser, then the Pin logo to keep OllaCart handy.

3. Visit OllaCart.com to Sign Up.

OllaCart.com only requires you to sign in with an email. The extension will automatically link to the account that is signed in.

4. Start Online Shopping.

This is the fun part! Visit any online store or website, ranging from the most obscure sites to amazon, and add it to your cart! All you need to do is make sure you are collecting the correct information for your product with our extension.

5. Find a Product you are considering?

Want to share this product with your friends and get some feedback? Maybe you know you will want this product at another time? Just like the way it looks? See someone cool wearing this?

6. Select OllaCart logo from Chrome Search Bar.

Thats the beauty of it.

7. Hover over product image.

Look at the message in the bottom left corner and confirm that it matches your desired addition to OllaCart.

8. Click image when the description is correct.

Hovering over and then selecting the image will automatically add the product or item to your OllaCart.

9. Visit OllaCart to view saved item.

Go to OllaCart.com to see your selected item. Your selection will allow you to share your list of items with others via a public url and maintain a list of products you are interested in across all of the internet.

10. On OllaCart, select the item so that the border turns blue.

This shares your item to your pubic OllaCart web page, allowing you to share multiple items at a time.

11. Select Share Logo to view public list of items.

This will populate the item to share into your public website for sharing. You can change it any time.

12. Copy and paste your personal link to share your cart with others.

Click on the share logo to get to your public OllaCart at any time. To share your page, copy and paste the link to any social media or via whichever method you prefer to share your OllaCart.

13. To delete items from your OllaCart, select the X in corner on private page.

This will remove them from your private and public OllaCart. To remove from just your shared page, remove the border by selecting the item on your private OllaCart.

14. To visit the source of the items you added to cart, select the link logo.

OllaCart keeps all your items in one place, and makes it easy to get back to where you were shopping.

15. To create another account, add or remove punctuation from your email.
